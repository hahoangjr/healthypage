How to use this

Track your morning body weight and waist 3–7 times per week, then look at weekly averages. If your weight is not moving after 2–3 weeks, adjust calories by about 100–200 kcal/day. Try to keep lifting performance stable during fat loss.

This website gives general education only. It is not medical advice. For medical conditions, eating disorders, pregnancy, diabetes, kidney disease, or medication interactions, ask a physician or registered dietitian.

BMI categories follow common adult screening ranges. BMI is useful but imperfect because it does not directly measure body composition.
